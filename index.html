Manhattan Address System

  • Streets run east/west.
  • Avenues run north/south.
  • Caveat: We will focus on the area between 14th and 59th. The same system applies throughout the whole island, but there are some complexties in other parts that we want to avoid.
Streets Avenues
Direction east / west north / south
Count ~200 ~20
Numbers increase south → north east → west
Finding an address straightforward needs a formula and a "magic number"

Street addresses

Buildings start at 1 at either direction of 5th ave and fan out. Let's take 22nd street as an example:

  • The first block east of 5th Ave starts at 1E 22nd St
  • The first block west of 5th Ave starts at 1W 22nd St

West of 5th Ave (W addresses)

Avenue Block Building Number Range
5th → 6th Avenue 1–99 W
6th → 7th Avenue 100–199 W
7th → 8th Avenue 200–299 W
8th → 9th Avenue 300–399 W
9th → 10th Avenue 400–499 W

East of 5th Ave (E addresses)

Avenue Block Building Number Range
5th → Park Ave (used to be called 4th ave) 1–99
Park Ave (used to be called 4th ave) → 3rd Ave 100–199
3rd → 2nd Ave 200–299
2nd → 1st Ave 300–399

Examples

523 Third Ave

Drop 3 -> 52. Divide by 2 -> 26. Add Third Ave's 10. Result: near 36th St.

501 Fifth Ave

Drop 1 -> 50. Divide by 2 -> 25. Fifth Ave in this range adds 18. Result: about 43rd St.

78 Riverside Dr

Special case: drop 8 -> 7, then add 72. Result: about 79th St.

129 W 81st St

This is already a numbered street address. West means west of Fifth Avenue; 81st tells you the cross street.

2. Queens Address System

Queens addresses usually have three fields: cross-street field, house-number field, and street-name field. The hyphen matters.

43 cross street - 12 house number 47th Avenue street name
1

Streets, Places, and Lanes usually run north-south

They generally increase as you move east. If extra roads are squeezed in, the order is Street, then Place, then Lane.

2

Avenues, Roads, Drives, and Terraces usually run east-west

They generally increase as you move south. If extra roads are squeezed in, the order is Avenue, Road, Drive, then Terrace.

3

The house number resets after major cross streets

The number after the hyphen often starts near 01 on a new block. Odd numbers are usually on one side, even numbers on the other.

Examples

21-23 15th Avenue

On 15th Avenue, between 21st and 22nd Streets. The house number on that block is 23.

15-23 156th Street

On 156th Street, between 15th and 16th Avenues. Same house number, different street family.

156-23 15th Avenue

Could be around the corner from 15-23 156th Street because the fields swap roles.

15-23 156th Avenue

This is not close to 15-23 156th Street. Same first two fields, totally different Avenue.
